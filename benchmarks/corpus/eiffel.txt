The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower in Paris, France. It was designed by the engineering company of Gustave Eiffel and completed in 1889. The Eiffel Tower stands 330 meters tall including its antennas. It was built as the entrance arch for the 1889 World's Fair. The Eiffel Tower receives around 7 million visitors every year, making it one of the most visited paid monuments in the world.
